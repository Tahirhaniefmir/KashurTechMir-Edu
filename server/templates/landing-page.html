About Kashur System

Kashur System is an educational platform dedicated to helping students up to Class 12 (NCERT & CBSE) in Jammu & Kashmir. We provide quality notes, previous year papers (free of cost), project guidance, lab training, and skill-based courses to help students succeed academically and practically.
